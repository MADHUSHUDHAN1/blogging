Digital Marketing Overview

Digital Marketing can be referred as the subset of Marketing,
which utilizes internet, interlink able devices such as desktop,
laptop,tablet, and smartphone etc. and various online digital platforms
to promote products and services of different businesses.

Digital Marketing Component

1. Search Engine Marketing

Search Engine Marketing or SEM can be define as the
process of gaining website traffic by purchasing advertisements
on search engines. There are many search engine marketing platforms,
in which Google AdWords, Bing AdWords and Yahoo: Seach Ads are some popular
search engine marketing platforms.

So, the greatest strenth of SEM is that it offers
advertisers the opportunity to put their add in front of
right customers who want to buy the product or service at some instant.

2. Social Media Marketing

Social Media Marketing(SNM) can be define as the marketing
refers to process of gaining traffic or attention through social media
platforms. And in this era of internet there is no one who is hidden from it.

The top most popular social media platforms are LinkedIn
Instagram,Pinterest,Facebook,Twitter,Snapchat and YouTube.

3.Search Engine Optimization

SEO or Searh Engine Optimization can be defined as the
process of getting traffic from the "Free","organic","editorial"
or "natural" search results on search engines.

There are three types of SEO techniques:

  • White Hat
  • Black Hat
  • Grey Hat

4. Content Marketing

Content marketing is a strategic marketing approach
focused on creating and distributing valuable, relevant,
and consistent content (infographics, reports, eBooks, e-mail,
blogs and videos) to attract and retain a clearly defined audience to
drive profitable customer action.

Benefits of Content Marketing within enterprise are:

  • Increased Sales
  • Got loyal customers
  • Cost Saving

5. Email Marketing

Email marketing can be referred as the subset of digital
marketing which uses email to promote products and services.
But a better definition of email marketing can be delivered as the
used of email to develop relationships with potential customer or/and clients.

There are 94% users of internet use email, while only
61% users use social media platforms. And 75% of adult users prefer
email marketing over others amrketing methods. That's why we must
know about the marketing methods techniques.

6.Web Analytics

Web Analytics is the process of analysing the behaviour of
visitors to a website. Web analytics enable a business to attract more visitors.
It can be categorised into two

  • On-site web analytics
  • Off-site web analytics

7.Sales Funnel

The ultimate object of digital marketing techniques is to
bring potential customers and having sells for the respective
businesses. To create sales funnel, you have to have knowledge a
comprehensive digital marketing approach you need to know all the verticals
of digital marketing to create a profitable and successful sales funnel is when
someone goes through to go from traffic to repeat customers. It is a system that adds
and removes barrier for someone to become a customer to repeat customer when it is approprite

It can be simple or short or highly complex.
